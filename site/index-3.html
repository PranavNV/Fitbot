Curl Holds

Stand straight and upright. Hold a dumbbell in each hand with underhand grip. Hold the weights at arm’s length next to the sides of your body with palms facing forward. Raise right forearm until your elbow bends 90-degree or until your forearm is parallel to the ground. Holding the right arm in that position, curl the dumbbell with left arm. Continue to curl the left dumbbell until it reaches the level of your shoulder. Return to the starting position by lowering the left dumbbell. Switch arms and perform this exercise with opposite arms (i.e. holding the left arm static, curl the right dumbbell)
